Charities List

Explore the incredible work of the following charities, each making a positive impact on communities around the world. Learn more about their missions and how you can help as a volunteer.

Saint Joseph

Founded: 1995

Mission: Saint Joseph charity is dedicated to providing food, shelter, and community support for families in need. They focus on empowering vulnerable individuals and offering a path to stability through housing, education, and healthcare services.

Areas of Impact: Nationwide outreach, assisting homeless families, food banks, and local community development.

Make-A-Wish Foundation

Founded: 1980

Mission: The Make-A-Wish Foundation grants seriously ill children the chance to fulfill their most heartfelt wish, whether it's meeting a celebrity, visiting a dream destination, or participating in a special event. Their work brings hope, strength, and joy to children in difficult circumstances.

Areas of Impact: Nationwide (United States), supporting children with life-threatening conditions and their families through wish experiences.

Children International

Founded: 1936

Mission: Children International helps children in poverty from an early age, offering comprehensive support through education, health services, and life skills training. Their focus is to break the cycle of poverty for children and families globally.

Areas of Impact: Global reach, with a focus on education, nutrition, healthcare, and family support in developing countries.
